An expense tracker system is a software application that allows users to track and manage their personal and/or business expenses. This type of system typically provides a user interface to enter, view, and update expense data. The system may include features such as categorization and reporting, budgeting, and integration with other financial systems. Expense tracking systems can be used to monitor spending, help users identify cost savings opportunities, and provide insights into budgeting decisions. Expense tracking systems may also be used to track employee expenses, such as business travel and entertainment expenses.
